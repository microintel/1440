About This App

The Idea:

Every single day, you are given exactly 1,440 minutes. You cannot save them for tomorrow, buy more, or get them back. This app acts as a visual clock to remind you that time goes by quickly, and every minute counts.

How to use it:

  • Live Timer: The main circle counts down how many minutes you have left before the day ends.
  • Plan Your Day: Go to the Profile tab to choose how you want to spend your day. You can add your own daily activities like sleep, college, or hobbies.
  • 24-Hour Balance: To keep things accurate, all your daily activities must add up to exactly 24 hours.
